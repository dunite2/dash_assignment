About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of data:

  • URLs: Direct links to websites
  • Text: Plain text messages
  • Contact Info: vCard format for adding contacts
  • WiFi Credentials: WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;;
  • Email: mailto:someone@example.com
  • Phone Numbers: tel:+1234567890
  • SMS: sms:+1234567890

Example Data Formats:

Website: https://www.example.com
WiFi: WIFI:T:WPA;S:MyNetwork;P:MyPassword;;
Email: mailto:contact@example.com
Phone: tel:+1-555-123-4567
SMS: sms:+1-555-123-4567
Contact: BEGIN:VCARD
VERSION:3.0
FN:John Doe
TEL:+1-555-123-4567
EMAIL:john@example.com
END:VCARD
